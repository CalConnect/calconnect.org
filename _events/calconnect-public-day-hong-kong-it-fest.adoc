==== About the Event

image:{{'/assets/images/public_day_logo_0.jpg' | relative_url }}[width=800, height=464]

On behalf of our conference hosts – Hong Kong’s Office of the Government Chief Information Officer and Hong Kong-based Ribose – CalConnect invites you to attend our all-day event at the Hong Kong International ITFest on April 20th, 2016. The event will be held at the Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre.

=== Key Highlights

* Keynote by Hong Kong’s CIO, Ir. Allen Yeung.
* Updates on Calendaring & Scheduling standards.
* Insights into CalConnect membership and its benefits.
* Networking with CalConnect members and developers.

=== Background

CalConnect, the Calendaring & Scheduling Consortium, is a partnership between vendors and users of calendaring tools. It promotes interoperability and advances the development of standards through its Technical Committees. While not a formal standards body, CalConnect has been a hub for calendaring standards discussions over the past five years.
