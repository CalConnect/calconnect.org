== CalConnect XIV - February 2-6, 2009
Microsoft, Redmond, Washington

image::/assets/images/MicrosoftRedmond.jpg[Microsoft, Redmond, Washington, width=800, height=533]

== Event Overview

CalConnect XIV includes:
- *CalConnect Interoperability Test Event*: February 2–5, 2009
- *CalConnect Roundtable XIV (Members Meeting)*: February 4–6, 2009

[.date-display-range]
Monday, February 2, 2009 to Friday, February 6, 2009

*Recent Changes:*  
_Information current to January 22, 2009_  
- Schedule Change Updates
- Dinner Locations

CalConnect XIV is hosted by link:https://web.archive.org/web/20090126053702/http://www.microsoft.com/[Microsoft].  
The IOP Testing will occur Monday to Wednesday morning, followed by the Roundtable starting Wednesday lunch through Friday afternoon.  

[NOTE]
====
You do *not* need finished or polished code to participate in the IOP testing. Early testing with other participants can assist in debugging and development.
====

*Key Highlights:*
- *CalConnect Interoperability Test Event*: For members and non-members (participants or observers).
- *Roundtable XIV*: Includes presentations, technical committee sessions, BOFs (Birds of a Feather sessions), and the Plenary meeting.
