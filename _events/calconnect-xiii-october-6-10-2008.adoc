== CalConnect XIII - October 6-10, 2008

image::link/assets/images/Yahoo.jpg[Yahoo!, 750, 444]

== Event Details
- *Dates:* Monday, October 6 – Friday, October 10, 2008
- *Location:* Yahoo! Mission College Campus, 2821 Mission College Blvd, Santa Clara, CA 95054, USA
- *Host:* Yahoo!

== Introduction
CalConnect XIII is composed of two key events: 
- The CalConnect Interoperability (IOP) Test Event, held from Monday to Wednesday morning.
- The CalConnect Roundtable (Members Meeting), starting Wednesday at 12:30 PM.

- *Timing:* Sessions begin daily at 08:00 and end at 17:30.
- *Dress Code:* Casual to business casual.
